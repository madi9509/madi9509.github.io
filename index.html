Mindful

healthy mind, healthy life

We are a bimonthly magazine and daily website that aims to report on all forms of applied mindfulness—in healthcare, education, science, relationships, the business world, arts and culture, athletics, and home life. Our definition of mindfulness is broad but rigorous. We are looking for stories—journalistic reportage, profiles, essays—that illustrate how mindfulness can enrich our lives, the lives of those around us, and shape our world for the better.

Do you have an idea to share with Mindful readers? Read on to learn how to submit articles for consideration and what Mindful is looking for in submissions.
